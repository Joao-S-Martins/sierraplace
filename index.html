Apartments for Rent in Porterville, CA

Sierra Place offers apartments for rent in Porterville, California. Conveniently located in downtown Porterville, Sierra Place offers all the luxurious conveniences of home with a premier location. Our community offers one and two bedroom apartments for rent in Porterville. With four different floor plan styles, we will surely have a home to fit your living needs!

 

Our community is tucked in a quaint neighborhood setting yet conveniently located next to shopping and dining. Set in an area with beautiful landscaping, enjoy an afternoon outside next to our sparkling, resort-style swimming pool. Take advantage of spacious kitchens with sliding glass doors Homes feature central air conditioning, washer and dryer units in most homes, covered parking options and large bedrooms with ample natural lighting. We also offer pet friendly apartments and even accept most large breeds, so be sure to bring your four-legged friend with you!
